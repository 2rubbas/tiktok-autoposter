Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Howard Clip Autoposter ("the App") is a personal-use application operated by an individual ("the Operator"). This policy explains how data is handled.

1. Data we access

The App uses the TikTok API with the Operator's authorization to publish video content to the Operator's own TikTok account. It accesses only the Operator's own basic account information and posting endpoints, solely to perform uploads.

2. Data we store

The App stores the access and refresh tokens required to post on the Operator's behalf. These are kept locally on the Operator's own computer and are not shared with any third party.

3. Data we do not collect

The App does not collect, process, or store data belonging to any other TikTok users. It is not used to access other users' content or personal information.

4. Data sharing

No data is sold or shared with third parties. Data is transmitted only between the App and TikTok's official API.

5. Retention and deletion

Stored tokens can be revoked at any time by the Operator through their TikTok account settings, after which the App can no longer access the account.
